Gameplay Showcase

Rhythm-driven combat meets narrative depth in this top-down action crawler

The Protagonists

Vincent

"Brave defender of justice and love"

A passionate warrior who channels fire through rhythmic sword strikes. His symbiotic pact manifests as blazing area attacks that crescendo with the beat.

Combat Style:

Sword slash (steerable mid-animation)

AOE fire-cone on 4th consecutive beat

Pattern: b - b - b - S

Valerie

"Repressed potential, determined soul"

Insecure yet resolute, she weaponizes her tears into magical projectiles. Her sorrow transforms into power through contratempo timing.

Combat Style:

Shoots tears on off-beats

Forward torrent of magic tears (aimable)

Pattern: -b -b -b S (contratempo)

Vin

"Aggressive, violent, rudely funny"

Raw aggression personified. His heavy strikes knock enemies back while his spinning attacks clear crowds with brutal efficiency.

Combat Style:

Slow melee with knockback (1 beat)

2-beat spin AOE (mobile)

Pattern: b(1) - b(3) | b(1) - S(3-4)

Narrative Concept

Demon Doors

Infected citizens harbor portals to the underworld within their minds, corrupting them from within.

Symbiotic Powers

Our protagonists gained their abilities through pacts with lesser devils who oppose Asmodeus's invasion.

Mind Infiltration

Enter corrupted minds to battle demonic forces and shut the doors, freeing the victims.

Soul Price

Each hero sacrificed part of their soul in exchange for the power to fight back against the invasion.

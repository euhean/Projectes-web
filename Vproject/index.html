Gameplay Showcase

Rhythm-driven combat meets narrative depth in this top-down action crawler

The Protagonists

Vincent

"Brave defender of justice and love"

A passionate warrior who channels fire through rhythmic sword strikes. His symbiotic pact manifests as blazing area attacks that crescendo with the beat.

Valerie

"Repressed potential, determined soul"

Insecure yet resolute, she weaponizes her tears into magical projectiles.

Vin

"Aggressive, violent, rudely funny"

Raw aggression personified. Heavy strikes and crowd control.

Narrative Concept

Demon Doors

Infected citizens harbor portals to the underworld within their minds.

Symbiotic Powers

Pacts with lesser devils grant abilities at a cost.

Mind Infiltration

Enter corrupted minds to shut the doors and free the victims.

Soul Price

Each hero paid a portion of their soul for power.
